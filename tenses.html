English Tenses

Learn all 12 English tenses with easy rules, formulas and examples.

Present Tenses

Present Simple

Formula:
Subject + V1 + Object

Example:
I play football.
She goes to school.

Present Continuous

Formula:
Subject + is/am/are + Verb+ing

Example:
I am reading a book.
They are playing cricket.

Present Perfect

Formula:
Subject + has/have + V3

Example:
She has finished her homework.
We have eaten lunch.

Past Tenses

Past Simple

Formula:
Subject + V2 + Object

Example:
I played football.
She went to school.

Past Continuous

Formula:
Subject + was/were + Verb+ing

Example:
I was reading a book.
They were playing cricket.

Past Perfect

Formula:
Subject + had + V3

Example:
She had finished her homework.
We had eaten lunch.

Future Tenses

Future Simple

Formula:
Subject + will + V1

Example:
I will play football.
She will go to school.

Future Continuous

Formula:
Subject + will be + Verb+ing

Example:
I will be reading a book.
They will be playing cricket.

Future Perfect

Formula:
Subject + will have + V3

Example:
She will have finished her homework.
We will have eaten lunch.

Practice Sentences

Present Simple: I read books every evening.

Present Continuous: She is studying now.

Past Simple: We visited Lahore last week.

Past Continuous: They were watching TV at 8 PM.

Future Simple: I will call you tomorrow.

Future Perfect: By next year, he will have finished college.
