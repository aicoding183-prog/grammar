A–Z English Dictionary

Learn English words from A to Z with meanings and examples.

Letter A

Apple

Noun

A sweet fruit.

Examples:

  • I eat an apple every day.
  • She put a red apple in her bag.
  • The apple tasted sweet and crisp.
  • He shared his apple with his friend.
  • An apple is a healthy snack.

Letter B

Book

Noun

A set of written pages.

Examples:

  • I read a book every night.
  • She borrowed a book from the library.
  • The book has many colorful pictures.
  • He wrote his name on the book cover.
  • Books help us learn new things.

Examples:

  • The cat is sleeping on the sofa.
  • My cat loves to chase a ball.
  • She feeds the cat every morning.
  • The cat has soft white fur.
  • He heard the cat meow at night.

  • The dog is barking loudly.
  • My dog loves to play in the park.
  • She took the dog for a walk.
  • The dog sat beside his owner.
  • He gave the dog a tasty snack.

  • He does not eat fast food often.
