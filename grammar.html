English Grammar

Learn grammar step by step with simple explanations, examples, and practice exercises.

Nouns

Definition

A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing or idea.

Examples

Ali
School
Book
Cat
Pakistan

Types

✔ Proper Noun
✔ Common Noun
✔ Collective Noun

Pronouns

Definition

A pronoun is used instead of a noun.

Examples

He
She
They
We
It

Types

✔ Personal Pronouns
✔ Possessive Pronouns

Verbs

Definition

A verb is a word that shows an action or a state.

Examples

Run
Eat
Write
Sleep
Play

Sentence

Ali runs every morning.
She writes a letter.

Adverbs

Definition

An adverb describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

Examples

Quickly
Slowly
Carefully
Beautifully
Happily

Sentence

He runs quickly.
She sings beautifully.

Adjectives

Definition

An adjective describes a noun.

Examples

Happy
Small
Beautiful
Blue

Sentence

She has a beautiful dress.
The small cat is sleeping.

Prepositions

Definition

A preposition shows the relationship between words.

Examples

In
On
Under
Behind

Sentence

The book is on the table.
The cat is under the chair.

Conjunctions & Articles

Conjunctions

Words like and, but, or join ideas.

Articles

a, an, and the are used before nouns.

Example

I want tea and coffee.
She is an honest girl.

Sentence Structure

Basic Pattern

Subject + Verb + Object

Example

Ali eats an apple.

Tip

Start with short sentences and add one detail at a time.

Tenses

Present

I study every day.

Past

I studied yesterday.

Future

I will study tomorrow.

Quick Practice Tips

Read

Read simple English stories to notice grammar patterns.

Write

Write 3-5 sentences daily using new grammar rules.

Speak

Practice speaking aloud to build confidence and fluency.
